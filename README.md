# shusher.github.io
Shush your surroundings


## Why
It allows you to monitor your surroundings and automatically alert your noisy neighbours. Perfect for open spaces and meetings when people like yelling more than listening.

## How it works
Give access to your mic through the browser
Adjust the threshold to a reasonable to you level (keep in mind if you are near the PC you would spike it more easily)
Enjoy
